African Community Education

"EMBRACING CULTURE.
BUILDING COMMUNITY.
CREATING CHANGE.
At African Community Education we empower African refugee and immigrant youth through educational, leadership, and cultural programming. We believe in a customized approach to meet the educational needs of each student to achieve better results."

– AfricanCommunityEducation.org

West Philadelphia Achievement Charter Elementary School

During May Experiential Learning Program in 2023, I went to West Philadelphia Achievement Charter Elementary School. Understanding students there are suffering from gang violence, poverty, and food insecurity, I use care to enhance their educational and life experience. The energy that live within each one of the students sparked me for continuing with education. Therefore, I connected African Community Education with Rectory to help more people with education.
