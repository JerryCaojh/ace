West Philadelphia Achievement Charter Elementary School

During May Experiential Learning Program in 2023, I went to West Philadelphia Achievement Charter Elementary School. Understanding students who are suffering from gang violence, poverty, and food insecurity, I use care to enhance their educational and life experience. The energy that live within each one of the students sparked me to continue with education. Therefore, I connected African Community Education with Rectory to help more people with education.
